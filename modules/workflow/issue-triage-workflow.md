# Issue Triage Workflow

## Purpose

Turn incoming problems and requests into clear next actions.

## Steps

1. Capture the report or request.
2. Identify affected users, versions, and environments.
3. Classify impact and urgency.
4. Check existing docs, known issues, and roadmap.
5. Decide action: fix, investigate, document, defer, or close.
6. Assign owner or next step.
7. Update known issues or roadmap.

## Output

- Severity or priority.
- Reproduction or acceptance criteria.
- Owner and next action.
- Documentation or memory updates.
